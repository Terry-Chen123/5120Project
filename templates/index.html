    Babies & Young children


    In babies and children younger than five years:
    pneumonia is more likely to be caused by a virus.

    In children older than five years:
    pneumonia is more likely to be caused by bacteria.

    About children pneumonia

    Pneumonia is a lung infection.

    When someone has pneumonia, the small airways in their lungs
    get clogged with mucus.

    This reduces the amount of oxygen that can get into their body.
